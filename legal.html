Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 22, 2026
1. Our Commitment

Mystery Bite is committed to ensuring that Chinese culinary exploration is inclusive for everyone, including those with visual, auditory, or motor impairments.

2. Standards & Goal

We strive to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Our development prioritizes human-centric design.

3. Features Implemented
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: All interactive elements and food cards contain descriptive accessibilityLabels compatible with VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android).
  • Dynamic Type Support: The App respects system-wide font size settings, ensuring text scales legibly without breaking the layout.
  • Haptic & Auditory Feedback: Unique vibration patterns and sound cues signal the rarity of the "Blind Box" opening result, assisting users with low vision.
  • Touch Target Optimization: Interactive buttons differ in shape and have a minimum touch area of 44x44 points.
  • Color Contrast: Text and UI elements are designed to meet the 4.5:1 contrast ratio standard.
4. Continuous Improvement

We are continuously working to improve accessibility, including future updates for:

  • AI-generated alt-text for complex food illustrations.
  • High-contrast themes.
