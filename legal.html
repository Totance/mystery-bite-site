Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 18, 2026
1. Our Commitment

At Mystery Bite, we believe that the joy of discovering Chinese culinary culture should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities. We are committed to ensuring that our digital "blind box" experience is inclusive and user-friendly for all our users, including those with visual, auditory, or motor impairments.

2. Standards & Goal

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our development process, powered by innovative AI technology and guided by human-centric design, prioritizes accessibility from the ground up.

3. Features Implemented

To provide an inclusive experience, Mystery Bite includes the following accessibility features:

  • Screen Reader Compatibility: We provide descriptive accessibilityLabels for all interactive elements, including our hand-drawn food illustrations and rarity indicators, ensuring compatibility with VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android).
  • Dynamic Type Support: The App supports system-wide font scaling. Users can increase text size without losing functionality or encountering layout overlaps.
  • Haptic & Auditory Feedback: For users with low vision, we provide unique vibrations (haptic feedback) and sound cues during the "blind box" opening sequence to signal success and rarity.
  • Touch Target Optimization: All interactive buttons and cards are designed with a minimum touch area of 44x44 points to assist users with limited fine motor skills.
  • Color Contrast: We carefully curate our color palettes to ensure text-to-background contrast ratios meet or exceed the 4.5:1 standard for readability.
4. Continuous Improvement

As an independent designer-led project, we recognize that accessibility is an ongoing journey. We are constantly working to improve the App’s features and compatibility with assistive technologies.

We are currently working on:

  • Enhanced AI-driven image descriptions for all food gallery items.
  • High-contrast mode toggles in the Settings menu.
5. Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Mystery Bite. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions on how we can improve, please reach out to us:

  • Email: Langyuetan@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to accessibility inquiries within 3 business days.
6. Formal Complaint Process

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility-related inquiry, you may contact us to escalate the issue. We are dedicated to finding a solution that allows you to enjoy our app to the fullest.
